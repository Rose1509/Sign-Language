Aa

How to sign

  1. Make a fist with your dominant hand.
  2. Keep your thumb on the side of your fingers, not sticking out.
  3. Ensure your fingers are curled tightly into your palm.
  4. Palm faces outward (toward the person you are signing to).
  5. Keep your hand steady while signing.
